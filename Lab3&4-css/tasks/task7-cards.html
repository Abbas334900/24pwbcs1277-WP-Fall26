Summary

This Card Component Design showcase demonstrates:

  • Product Cards: With images, prices, ratings, and CTAs
  • Service Cards: Icons, descriptions, and feature lists
  • Team Member Cards: Profile information and social links
  • Blog Cards: Article previews with metadata
  • Consistent Spacing: Padding, margins, and gaps
  • Shadow Effects: For visual depth and hierarchy
  • Hover Transformations: Scale, translate, and opacity changes
  • Image Effects: Gradients and zoom on hover
  • Call-to-Action Buttons: Primary and secondary actions
  • Badges and Metadata: Additional information display
  • Responsive Design: Grid layouts that adapt to screen size
